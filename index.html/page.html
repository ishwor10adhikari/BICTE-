BICTE Program - Tribhuvan University

Faculty of Education

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Information Communication Technology Education (BICTE) is a 4-year program under Tribhuvan University, focusing on integrating technology with education. This program prepares students for careers in both computer science and educational technology.

Key Features

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Total Credits: 138 credit hours
  • Focus Areas: Communication skills, educational theory, ICT specialization, and teaching internship

Core Areas of Study

  • Programming (C/C++, Java)
  • Database Management
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Educational Technology Integration

Career Prospects

Graduates can work as educators, software developers, system administrators, network administrators, or continue with advanced studies in ICT. Educational technology specialists and curriculum developers are also in high demand.

Eligibility

Students must have completed Grade 12 or an equivalent degree with a minimum of a second division or a C+ grade in aggregate (2.01 GPA or higher).
